INTRO

"A sleep-deprived addict, in a toxic relationship, teeters on the edge of self-destruction when his desperate need for a fix leads him down a path that puts his moral compass to the test."

Come Down is a contemporary Neo-Noir/Thriller, which focusses on the struggles of addiction and substance abuse, following a protagonist that wants to change in a world that won't let him.

COME DOWN 01 ↓

TONE

Films that inspired Come Down are Good Time (2017, Safdie Brothers), Taxi Driver (1976, Martin Scorsese) and Pulp Fiction (1994, Quentin Tarantino), along with the absurd black comedy found in a variety of Coen brother films.

The story of Come Down is told through an intense POV, with noir styled voice over. We never leave the mind of our protagonist, Donnie Malone, through a journey that takes place over a 24 hour period.

"...That's the thing about Adderall... It makes you fucking numb....

....I couldn't cry if I wanted to... I hit the fucking jackpot....

....I got everything I ever wanted.... but her."

COME DOWN 02 ↓

VISUALS

Come Down will have a dark, gritty and colorful visual style, similar to the many classics in the Neo-Noir/Thriller genre.

It will be digitally shot in an aspect ratio of 2.39 and embrace the colors and grain of Kodak 35mm motion picture film.
